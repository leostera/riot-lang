---
title: "Lockfiles"
description: "Why Riot lockfiles exist and what role they play in repeatable package workflows."
---

Lockfiles exist so dependency resolution becomes a recorded fact rather than a fresh surprise on every
machine.

## What they buy you

- repeatable builds
- reviewable dependency movement
- less ambient uncertainty about what the package graph actually is

## How to think about them

The manifest says what the package wants.

The lockfile says what Riot actually selected for this workspace state.

That distinction is what keeps package work understandable once the graph gets nontrivial.
